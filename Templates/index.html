Welcome to Rajeev Gandhi Memorial College of Engineering & Technology (RGMCET)

At RGMCET, we are dedicated to providing an exceptional learning experience that prepares our students for a bright future. We offer a variety of programs, state-of-the-art facilities, and a dynamic campus life that fosters growth, innovation, and leadership.

About RGMCET

Established in Nandyal, Andhra Pradesh, RGMCET is a prestigious institution committed to excellence in engineering education. Our college is known for its world-class infrastructure, experienced faculty, and a culture that encourages research and innovation. We strive to nurture the best talents and transform them into professionals who can excel in their chosen fields.
